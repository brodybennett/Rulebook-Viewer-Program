# Names and Honorific Titles of Mystical Deities and Angels {#names-and-honorific-titles}

> **Lore:** Some of the following content is cited from Baidu Baike.

- See also: [[Gods and Faith]]

## How to Read This Reference {#names-honorific-how-to-read}

- **Honorific Name:** The formal epithet(s) listed for a deity/existence.
- **Sacred Emblem:** The church badge/emblem description associated with a deity.
- **Symbols:** Common iconography associated with a deity.

## Seven Orthodox Gods {#names-honorific-seven-orthodox-gods}

### Goddess of the Night {#names-honorific-goddess-of-the-night}

- Pathway: [[MISSING REF: Darkness Pathway]]
- Honorific Name: Goddess of the Night, more exalted than the starry sky, more ancient than eternity; Mother of Concealment; Empress of Misfortune and Fear; Lord of Repose and Silence.
- Additional:
  - Before the God War, there should be an additional clause before “Mother of Concealment,” namely “Lord of Crimson.”
- Symbols: Night and the stars, or the crimson moon.
- Church Sacred Emblem: Darkness Sacred Emblem — a deep-black base, adorned with brilliant specks, surrounding exactly half of a crimson moon.

### Lord of Storms {#names-honorific-lord-of-storms}

- Pathway: [[Tyrant Pathway]]
- Honorific Name: King of the Sky, Emperor of the Sea, Lord of Natural Disasters, God of Storms!
- Additional Version: The king who rules the sky, the emperor who commands the sea, the great God of Storms.
- Church Sacred Emblem: Storm Sacred Emblem — composed of symbols of raging wind, ocean waves, and thunder.

### God of Knowledge and Wisdom {#names-honorific-god-of-knowledge-and-wisdom}

- Pathway: [[Reader Pathway]]
- Honorific Name: The power of knowledge, the power of reason, God of Wisdom.
- Church Sacred Emblem: Knowledge and Wisdom Sacred Emblem — an open book and an all-knowing eye.

### Eternal Blazing Sun {#names-honorific-eternal-blazing-sun}

- Pathway: [[Sun Pathway]]
- Honorific Name: Eternal Blazing Sun, Undying Light, Embodiment of Order, God of Contracts, Protector of Commerce.
- Church Sacred Emblem: Sun Sacred Emblem — an abstract sun surrounded by segmented lines.

### God of Steam and Machinery {#names-honorific-god-of-steam-and-machinery}

- Pathway: [[Paragon Pathway]]
- Honorific Name (version 1): Embodiment of Essence, Protector of Craftsmen, the Radiance of Technology.
- Honorific Name (version 2): Great God of Steam and Machinery; You are the Embodiment of Essence; You are the Protector of Craftsmen; You are the Radiance of Technology.
- Church Sacred Emblem: Triangle Sacred Emblem — a sturdy triangle filled with symbols such as steam, gears, and levers.

### Earth Mother {#names-honorific-earth-mother}

- Pathway: [[Earth Pathway]]
- Honorific Name (version 1): Thank you, Source of Life! Praise you, Mother of All Things!
- Honorific Name (version 2): Source of Life, Mother of All Things, Lord of Crimson (personal modification of the honorific name after the God War)
- Church Sacred Emblem: Life Sacred Emblem — a simple line-drawn infant surrounded by ears of wheat, flowers, and a spring-water symbol.

### God of Combat {#names-honorific-god-of-combat}

- Honorific Name: Symbol of strength and glory, great God of Knights, Lord of War, Patron of Firearms and Cannons, Twilight of All Things.
- Sacred Emblem: God of Combat Badge — a twilight symbol and a sword-shaped insignia.

## Secret Existences {#names-honorific-secret-existences}

> **GM Note:** Without a proper reason, and if a character’s knowledge of mysticism is insufficient, or if they are not a Beyonder, they should not know of these existences. Many are evil gods, may not respond to prayers, and differ significantly from the orthodox gods.

### True Creator {#names-honorific-true-creator}

- Pathway: [[Hanged Man Pathway]]
- Honorific Name: “The Lord who created everything, the Master behind the veil of shadows, the fallen nature of all living beings”
- Main Followers Belong To: Aurora Order
- Lore:
  - A frequently appearing deity associated with a terrifying organization; they often claim responsibility for events to expand their fame.
  - You may only know His name, and not the corresponding honorific name.

### The Fool {#names-honorific-the-fool}

- Pathway: [[Fool Pathway]] (Seer Pathway)
- Honorific Name: “The Fool that does not belong to this era, the mysterious ruler above the gray fog, the Yellow-and-Black King who wields good luck.”
- Main Followers Belong To: Tarot Club, Church of the Fool
- Note: Whether this deity exists depends on the module timeline.

### Primordial Witch {#names-honorific-primordial-witch}

- Pathway: [[MISSING REF: Witch Pathway]] (originally male)
- Honorific Name: (None for now)
- Main Followers Belong To: Witch Church (Witch Sect)
- GM Note: You may fabricate an honorific name based on its nature, as long as it points correctly.

### Cosmic Dark Side {#names-honorific-cosmic-dark-side}

- Pathway: [[Abyss Pathway]] (the only surviving ancient god; originally named the Demon King)
- Honorific Name: (None for now)
- Main Followers Belong To: Blood Worship Sect
- Note: This deity resides in the Abyss.
- GM Note: You may fabricate an honorific name based on its nature, as long as it points correctly.

### The Abyss {#names-honorific-abyss}

> **Lore:** The source of all evil, a land of corruption, the abode of demons.

- The most corrupting place— even angels will fall and lose control there.
- Those who guard the Abyss will ultimately be assimilated by it.
- The Cosmic Dark Side, by which even deities can be corrupted, is independent of the real world.
- Its entrance is in the Fog Sea.
- The present Abyss is a place where even most demons cannot survive.

### Emperor Roselle {#names-honorific-emperor-roselle}

- Pathway: [[Black Emperor Pathway]] (transmigrator)
- Status: Has fallen. Currently in the stage of imminent resurrection, and is sealed.
- Lore: Famous quote: “The taste of a witch is really not bad”

### Ancient Sun God {#names-honorific-ancient-sun-god}

- Pathway: [[Sun Pathway]] (Cornerstone) + [[Hanged Man Pathway]]
- Honorific Name:
  - Lord who created everything, the omniscient and omnipotent God;
  - You are the great source of all things; You are the beginning, and also the end;
  - You are the God of Gods; You are the ruler of the vast Astral World
- Note: After transcending Sequences, became a quasi–Old One; has now fallen.

### Visionary {#names-honorific-visionary-adam}

- Alias: Adam
- Pathway: [[Visionary Pathway]] (Spectator)
- Main Followers Belong To: Twilight Hermit Order

## Eight Ancient Gods {#names-honorific-eight-ancient-gods}

- Brief Introduction:
  - The eight ancient gods active during the Dark Epoch.
  - Due to the lack of the Sequence potion system brought by the Blasphemy Slate, most were deities of mixed pathways, and thus all were in a half-mad state.

### Giant King {#names-honorific-giant-king-ormir}

- Status: Has fallen.
- True Name: Ormir
- Pathway: [[MISSING REF: Twilight Giant Pathway]]
- Divine Kingdom: Giant King’s Court
- Brief Introduction:
  - Born to human parents.
  - The crown he once wore became the god-level Sealed Artifact Proof of Glory.

### Elf King {#names-honorific-elf-king-suniya-soliem}

- Status: Has fallen.
- True Name: Suniya Soliem
- Pathway: [[Tyrant Pathway]]
- Brief Introduction:
  - The first elf; led the clan away from the Western Continent to the Northern Continent.
  - Once suspected by Roselle and Klein to be one of the transmigrators.

### Sanguine Ancestor {#names-honorific-sanguine-ancestor-lilith}

- Status: Has fallen.
- True Name: Lilith
- Pathway: [[Moon Pathway]]
- Subordinate Deity: Goddess of Beauty Orniel (later defected to the Elf King; now fallen)
- Life Goddess: (after Lilith’s death; fallen)
- Main Followers Belong To: Sanguines (continue to exist to this day)
- Honorific Name (old honorific name; now cannot be directed):
  - “The one and only red moon;”
  - “Symbol of life and beauty;”
  - “Mother of all spiritual powers.”
- Notes:
  - Active in the Dark Epoch’s Era of the First Blaze.
  - Together with the Giant King and the Elf King, formed the humanoid-creature faction.
  - Originally, she retained the most sanity; was called the embodiment of beauty.
  - Later heavily influenced by the Fallen Mother Goddess as the primal barrier weakened.
  - At the end of the Era of the First Blaze, she fell due to a betrayal.

### Dragon of Imagination {#names-honorific-dragon-of-imagination-angerwede}

- Status: Has fallen, but resurrected under the identity of Aeriehogg.
- True Name: Angerwede
- Alias: King of Dragons, Dragon King
- Pathway: [[Visionary Pathway]] + other pathways
- Divine Kingdom: Liveseyd (City of Miracles)
- Brief Introduction: Once contaminated by God; imagined Groselle’s Travels and sealed the contamination.
- Subordinate Deity:
  - Nightmare Dragon Asulhode (fallen)
  - Wisdom Dragon Herabergen (has betrayed)

### Phoenix Ancestor {#names-honorific-phoenix-ancestor-gregrace}

- Status: Has fallen.
- True Name: Gregrace
- Pathway: [[Death Pathway]] + [[MISSING REF: Door Pathway]] (incomplete)
- Divine Kingdom: Calderon
- Notes:
  - Ancient Death; divine kingdom was originally the City of the Dead.
  - Later opened the Underworld within the spirit world and relocated the divine kingdom to the Underworld.
  - Opened a tributary for the River of Eternal Darkness, allowing indirect use of the River of Eternal Darkness.
  - In the Era of the First Blaze, with the Mutant King and the Dragon of Imagination, belonged to the non-humanoid-creature faction; opposed other ancient gods.
  - In the Twin Era, the faction collapsed.
  - At the end of the Dark Epoch, after being heavily wounded by the Ancient Sun God, it shattered and fell.
  - Before death, it threw the City of the Dead into the depths of the spirit world.
  - In the River of Eternal Darkness, there is a backup plan for resurrection.

### Mutant King {#names-honorific-mutant-king-kwasihtun}

- Status: Has fallen.
- True Name: Kwasihtun
- Pathway: [[MISSING REF: Chained Pathway]]
- Appearance: An ugly, twisted male, but he likes wrapping thorns and thorny roses around himself
- Notes:
  - Era of the First Blaze: With the Phoenix Ancestor and the Dragon of Imagination, belonged to the non-humanoid-creature faction; opposed other ancient gods.
  - In the late period, he was dragged to die together by the Sanguine Ancestor Lilith.

### Demon Wolf of Destruction {#names-honorific-demon-wolf-of-destruction-flegrea}

- Status: Has fallen.
- True Name: Flegrea
- Alias: Destroyer, King of Demon Wolves, Demon Wolf King, Night Demon Wolf
- Pathway: [[MISSING REF: Darkness Pathway]] + [[Fool Pathway]] (incomplete)
- Notes:
  - Held authority over night and concealment.
  - Active in the Dark Epoch’s Era of the First Blaze; a lone walker wanting to overturn all order and corrupt all living beings.
  - Never found the third Attendant of Mysteries characteristic.
  - Knew of the existence of Sefirah Castle among the Sefirot, but never entered it.
  - At the close of the Era of the First Blaze, with the Mutant King Kwasihtun, was dragged to die together by the Sanguine Ancestor Lilith.

### Demon King {#names-honorific-demon-king-farbauti}

- Status: Now active under the identity of the Cosmic Dark Side.
- True Name: Farbauti
- Pathway: [[Abyss Pathway]]
- Experience:
  - Did not ally with the other ancient gods; a lone walker wanting to overturn all order and corrupt all living beings.
  - Survived from the Dark Epoch to the present.
  - Active in the Black Iron Epoch under the identity of the Cosmic Dark Side.
