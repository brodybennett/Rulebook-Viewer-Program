# Sequence 0: Twilight Giant

## Names

- Also known as: "Giant", "Twilight"

## Nature

It can create matter; created matter inevitably decays, collapses, and perishes.

## Uniqueness

**Uniqueness** [[Uniqueness]] is manifested as the **Sword of Twilight** [[Sword of Twilight]].

- Appearance: a huge long sword covered with orange light.
