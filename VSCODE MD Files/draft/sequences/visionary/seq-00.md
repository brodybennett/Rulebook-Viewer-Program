# Visionary Pathway: Sequence 0

## Visionary

> **Lore:** The imaginary items will surely appear; the fantasy kingdom will come to the material world; and the declared future will be staged and become reality.

## Advancement

### Advancement Ritual

- **Advancement Ritual:** Let the development of the times conform to your expectations. At key points—within the prescribed trend of the times—take the [[Magic Potion]].

## Extraordinary Abilities

### Attribute Gain

- **Attribute Gain:** Intuition (INT) +3; Charisma +2; Strength +1; Constitution +1; Agility (DEX) +1.
- **Skill Increase:** [[Writing]] increases by 1 level.
