# Visionary Pathway: Sequence 2

## Discerner

## Advancement

### Advancement Ritual

- **Advancement Ritual (Unofficial):** Through accumulated observations, you must:
  - Understand the ideas of different classes, groups, and races in a city.
  - Know their subconscious needs and desires.
  - Deeply appreciate and understand subtle differences between different people and their lived experiences.
  - Grasp each person’s long-cherished wishes and their most sincere, essential thoughts—conscious or unconscious.
  - After you can almost predict all details of their lives, you drink the **potion** [[Potion]].

## Extraordinary Abilities

### Attribute Gain

- **Attribute Gain:** Intuition (INT) +2; Strength +1; Constitution +1; Agility (DEX) +1.
- Detection increases by 2 levels.
- [[Listening]] increases by 1 level.
