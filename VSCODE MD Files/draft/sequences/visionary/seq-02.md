# Sequence 2: Seeker

## Seeker

## Advancement

### Advancement Ritual

- **Advancement Ritual (Unofficial):** Through accumulated observations, you must:
  - Understand the ideas of different classes, groups, and races in a city.
  - Know their subconscious needs and desires.
  - Deeply appreciate and understand subtle differences between different people and their lived experiences.
  - Grasp each person’s long-cherished wishes and their most sincere, essential thoughts—conscious or unconscious.
  - After you can almost predict all details of their lives, you drink the **potion** [[LINK LATER: Potion | type=item | hint=Sequence 2 advancement]].

## Extraordinary Abilities

### Attribute Gain

- **Attribute Gain:** Inspiration +2; Strength +1; Constitution +1; Agility +1.
- [[LINK LATER: Detection | type=term | hint=skill]] increases by 2 levels.
- [[LINK LATER: Listening | type=term | hint=skill]] increases by 1 level.

### Insight

- **Effect:**
  - You can directly spy on the weakness in another person’s heart and perceive the deep interactions of the [[LINK LATER: Subconscious Sea | type=other | hint=Sea of subconscious mind]].
  - Often accompanied by a [[LINK LATER: Dream Weave | type=other | hint=psychic manifestation]] or [[LINK LATER: Psychic Vision | type=other | hint=psychic manifestation]], you can weave your foe’s worst fears. While fighting you, the foe takes:
    - -5 to all [[LINK LATER: Skill Checks | type=mechanic | hint=Core Rules]]
    - -3 to [[LINK LATER: Damage Checks | type=mechanic | hint=Core Rules]]
  - You can look for any [[LINK LATER: Inner World | type=other | hint=subconscious information]] information you need through the sea of the subconscious mind.
