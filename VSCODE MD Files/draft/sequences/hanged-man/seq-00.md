# Hanged Man Pathway: Sequence 0

## The Hanged Man

- See also: [[Hanged Man Pathway]]

- The **true god** in charge of the [[fallen realm]] holds partial authority over:
  - [[shadow]]
  - [[Darkness]]
  - [[corruption]]
  - [[filth]]
  - [[mutation]]
- In the dark, its divine power can create [[monsters]] out of thin air.
- It has the particularity of **[[resurrection]]**.
